What it is

One coordinated system — not another chat box to manage.

Most "AI assistants" hand you a blank chat box and leave the work to you. Jenius Systems is the opposite: a small team of specialized agents that already know my context, coordinate with each other, and act without being re-briefed every time.

A team, not a bot

Many specialized agents, each good at one thing, coordinated together — instead of one do-everything chatbot.

It remembers

Durable memory across what I tell it, so I'm never re-explaining context or repeating myself.

It acts ahead

Proactive by design — it sends the morning brief and the "you should know about this" nudges before I ask.

What it does

A handful of specialists, working as one.

Everything here runs today, reachable from one chat on Telegram.

Runs the day, proactively

A morning brief that fuses the schedule, the inbox and how I'm doing into one read — plus the follow-ups and "you should know about this" nudges, sent before I have to think about them.

Keeps the inbox under control

It reads and sorts what lands in the inbox, surfaces what actually needs me, and drafts replies — with a check-in before anything is sent.

Aware of the calendar

It reads the calendar, so its answers and its planning fit around what the week already looks like — no copy-pasting the day into a chat.

Looks out for how I'm doing

A daily read on readiness from the health data I already track, kept in a private log — so the rest of the day can flex to how rested I actually am.

Concierge for the real world

From "find me somewhere for dinner nearby" to planning the errand route around it — it does the legwork and hands back real options, not just chat.

Recipes & meals

Send it a recipe and it saves it to a personal recipe box and folds it into the food side of things — one place instead of scattered screenshots.

How it's built

Private by default. Careful by design.

It's private

A private setup that runs for one person. The data isn't sold or mined — it stays under my control.

Built as a fleet

Many specialized agents coordinated together do better than one do-everything bot — depth where it counts.

Careful with the risky stuff

Guardrails around anything sensitive or irreversible — it asks before it does something it can't undo.
